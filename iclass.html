ABOUT US

What do we do?

i-CLASS is a technology-based educational platform which offers tailored services to satisfy the needs of education stakeholders (Students, Teachers and Parents) by filling communication gaps. We provide controllable, value-for-money and comprehensive solutions that aim at facilitating contemporary learning ,especially during the secondary stage in Egypt and MENA region.

Who are we?

i-CLASS was established as an initiative from IT gurus, communication engineers, businessmen and educators who have natural passion for better education. Together we can achieve professional, efficient and intelligent educational environment for a brighter future.

What do we promise?

To always have the mentality of empowering students as a first priority, since we believe in the importance of enrolling smart technologies in education And to act as educational advocates by proposing reliable, custom-built, cost-efficient and up-to-date technologies that facilitate communication between students, parents and teachers.

What do we mean?

In our brand name (iClass), the (i) here stands for: All of our solutions are simply Intelligent On adding the (i) factor to the ordinary (Class), this leads to our purpose: replacing the typical physical classes experience with virtual, custom-made and intelligent ones.

Our Mission

Re-framing the educational market by providing superior, friendly, cost-efficient yet professional and dependable technological solutions for communications between students, teachers and parents .
